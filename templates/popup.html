Thank you

By receiving this card and playing this game with us, you are contributing to MT&L’s annual charitable gift. We understand a lot goes into keeping a community happy and healthy, so we would like to spread our donation across several charities this holiday season. Please suggest which organization you would like to receive a donation from MT&L on your behalf:

The Art Gallery of Nova Scotia — its two branches make art more accessible across the province.
The Beaverbrook Art Gallery — committed to enriching life through art.
The QEII Foundation — leading philanthropy, to bring better health care to all Atlantic Canadians.
Pathways (Spryfield) — helping students make it to high school graduation, and a brighter future.
PALS (Partners Assisting Local Schools) Program — linking New Brunswick businesses with students through powerful mentorships.

Thank you for playing and giving. MT&L wishes you the very best of the season, and a happy and healthy New Year.
